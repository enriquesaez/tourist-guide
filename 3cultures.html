Visit to the Three Cultures of Cordoba

  • All year available
  • Guide: Luis Larrea Barroso
  • Sinagoga Fortress of the Christian Monarchs Omeyan dynasty

This tour allows the visitor to admire the historical legacy left behind by each of the cultures which have left their mark in Cordoba over the ages in the impressive monuments built from de 8th century onwards.

The first stop on the route is the former Central Mosque of Qurtuba (Arabic Cordoba), built between the 8th and 10th centuries by the Omeyan dynasty in Al-Andalus. Next, we visit the Medieval synagogue (C. 14th), which is en the heart of the Jewish Quarter, and then end up at the 14th century Fortress of the Christian Monarchs, which was built by order of King Alfonso XI “the Avenger”.
