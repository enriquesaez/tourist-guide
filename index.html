Some of our tematic tours

  • BOOK

    Old Quarter of Cordoba

    The visit to the historic center of Cordoba includes the visit of the current Cathedral of Córdoba (former Mezquita-Aljama), as well as other squares and patios that make up its appearance.

  • BOOK

    Squares of Córdoba

    A deep look in which the visitor will perceive the transformation of the city throughout its History, its commercial activity, its people; in a pleasant tour of representative places.

  • BOOK

    The Three Cultures

    Tour that covers the historical legacy left by each one of the cultures that have passed through Córdoba since the 8th century. From the old Mosque-Aljama of Qurtuba, to the Alcazar de los Reyes Cristianos among other historic monuments.

  • BOOK

    Visit to Medina Azahara

    Millenary palatine city that begins to be built in the year 936 by the first Omeya caliph in Al-Andalus: Abd-Al-Rahman III. Medina Azahara was built for political and ideological purposes and as proof of power to the rest of the world.
