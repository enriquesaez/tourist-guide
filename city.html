Ways of visiting Cordoba

History and culture. Spell and Silence.

To my mind, there are two ways of visiting Cordoba: you either come for a quick look round and or come and soak it all in. There is nothing wrong with either way, of course, but I would argue that the latter way leaves a much more lasting impression on the visitor’s memory.

To talk about Cordoba is both easy and difficult. Easy, because there are thousands of details to dwell on; difficult, knowing what to focus on at any particular time. There is the history, culture, art, religion, customs, mysteries and monuments – the past is a priceless heritage and the present, a summary of everything that has gone on before. There are heroes, philosophers, monarchs and artists, an – let’s not forget – the common people.

Walking round Cordoba is a unique experience, which sets the senses reeling with emotions at the scents, the views, the flavours, the stones and the sounds – as well as the incomparable quality of its silence.

Cordoba is the result of centuries of integration of widely – different civilizations, cultures an religions, all of which flourished here, and more importantly, lived together in an atmosphere of tolerance. Each culture gave way to the next, inter – mixing an learning from each other, and together they created the Cordoba we know today, this wonderful city which is now yours to enjoy.
